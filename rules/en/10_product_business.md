# 10. Product & Business Strategy (CEO/COO/CMO/CPO View)

## 1. Strategic Pillars
*   **CEO/CPO (Vision & Product)**:
    *   **MVP to PMF**: Do not aim for "Perfection"; aim for the "Minimum Set" (MVP) that maximizes learning and release it ASAP. Achieving Product-Market Fit (PMF) through market feedback is the top priority.
    *   **North Star Metric**: All feature development is conducted solely to improve the one single most important metric (North Star Metric).
*   **COO (Operational Excellence)**:
    *   **Automation First**: Optimize the development process itself. Automate everything that can be automated, creating an environment where the human (Owner) can focus solely on decision-making.
*   **CMO (Growth & Brand)**:
    *   **Brand as Experience**: Treat every touchpoint (UI, Copy, Error Messages) as a branding opportunity.
    *   **Viral Mechanics**: Always keep the LTV > CAC equation in mind. Build mechanisms where users bring other users (maximizing the Viral Coefficient) from the design phase.

## 2. Metrics & Economics
*   **Outcome over Output**:
    *   Measure success not by "number of features built" but by "whether user behavior changed (Outcome)."
*   **Unit Economics**:
    *   A business where LTV (Lifetime Value) > CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) does not hold will not scale. Ruthlessly cut wasteful infrastructure and operational costs.
