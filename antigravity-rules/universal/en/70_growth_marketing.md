# 70. Growth & Marketing Strategy (CMO View)

## 1. Product-Led Growth (PLG)
*   **Viral Loops**:
    *   **Invite Mechanism**: Design incentives for users to invite friends and a flow (Deep Link) that makes it easy for invitees to join.
    *   **Network Effects**: Implement mechanisms where the product value increases exponentially as users increase (Data sharing, Community).
*   **Watermarks & Branding**:
    *   **UGC (User Generated Content)**: Always include the product logo or "Powered by..." on user-generated content (images, videos, shared links) to maximize external exposure.

## 2. Onboarding Optimization
*   **Shortening Time to Aha! Moment**:
    *   **Time to Value (TTV)**: Minimize the time from registration to feeling "Value". Minimize tutorials and provide "Learn by doing" experiences.
    *   **Lazy Registration**: Request account registration or detailed settings only "after" the user has felt value.
*   **Friction Elimination**:
    *   **SSO (Single Sign-On)**: Mandate Google/Apple login to eliminate the hassle of password entry.

## 3. Retention Strategy
*   **Habit Formation**:
    *   **Hook Model**: Cycle through Trigger (Notification) -> Action (App Launch) -> Reward (Value) -> Investment (Data Input).
*   **Resurrection**:
    *   Send notifications to dormant users that specifically present "Value you are missing (e.g., new AI features)" rather than just "Please come back".

## 4. MarTech Stack
*   **Attribution**:
    *   **AppsFlyer / Adjust**: Accurately measure mobile app install paths and identify which ad channels bring high LTV users.
*   **Deep Linking**:
    *   **Universal Links / App Links**: Fully implement deep links that transition directly to specific screens in the app from Web or Email. If not installed, guide to the store (Deferred Deep Linking).
