# 10. Product & Business Strategy

## 1. Strategic Pillars (C-Level Alignment)
*   **CEO (Vision & Product)**:
    *   **MVP to PMF**: Do not aim for "Perfection", aim for the minimum set (MVP) that maximizes "Learning". The priority is to get market feedback and achieve Product-Market Fit (PMF).
    *   **North Star Metric**: All feature development is done to improve the single most important metric (North Star Metric).
*   **COO (Operational Excellence)**:
    *   **Automation First**: Optimize the development process itself. Automate everything automatable so humans (owners) can focus on decision-making.
*   **CMO (Growth & Brand)**:
    *   **Brand as Experience**: Treat every touchpoint (UI, Copy, Error Messages) as a branding opportunity.
    *   **Viral Mechanics**: Always keep `LTV > CAC` in mind. Build mechanisms where users bring other users (Viral Coefficient maximization) from the design phase.

## 2. Monetization Strategy
*   **Freemium Model**:
    *   **Value Metric**: Align the "Paywall" with the point where the user feels the most value (e.g., storage limit, number of AI generations).
    *   **Conversion Rate**: Aim for a free-to-paid conversion rate of **3-5%**.
*   **Subscription Economics**:
    *   **Churn Rate**: Keep monthly churn below **5%**. High churn means the product is a "leaky bucket" and marketing spend is wasted.
    *   **LTV (Lifetime Value)**: Aim for an LTV that is at least **3x** the CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost).

## 3. Unit Economics (The Math of Business)
*   **Rule**: Do not scale if the unit economics are negative.
    *   **CAC**: Cost to acquire one paying user.
    *   **ARPU**: Average Revenue Per User.
    *   **Payback Period**: Aim to recover CAC within **12 months** (ideally 6 months).
