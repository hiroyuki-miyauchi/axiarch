# 71. Geo-Strategy & Localization

## 1. Tier Strategy
*   **Tier 1 (High Priority)**:
    *   **Targets**: North America, Japan, Western Europe, Australia.
    *   **Strategy**: Concentrate marketing budget as high ARPU is expected. Thoroughly culturalize (colors, images, phrasing) not just translate.
*   **Tier 2 (Growth)**:
    *   **Targets**: Southeast Asia, South America, India.
    *   **Strategy**: Aim for viral-led growth as user count is high but unit price is low. Optimize app size to be lightweight and work on low-spec devices.

## 2. Local Regulations
*   **Data Sovereignty**:
    *   **EU (GDPR)**: Store user data on servers within the EU (Frankfurt, Belgium, etc.) as much as possible.
    *   **China**: When entering the Chinese market, partner with local companies and use domestic servers (ICP License).
*   **Payments**:
    *   Support preferred payment methods in each country (Pix in Brazil, UPI in India, GrabPay in SE Asia) via Stripe etc. to prevent cart abandonment.

## 3. AIO (Artificial Intelligence Optimization)
*   **Brand Presence in AI**:
    *   Regularly check if your brand or product is correctly recognized within LLM training data (Brand Monitoring in AI).
    *   If AI outputs incorrect information (Hallucination), continue to publish correct information to encourage correction of training data.
